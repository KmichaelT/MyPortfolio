Regional Corporate Technologies (RCT) is an African software and technology solution company with its headquarters in South Sudan, although it operates throughout the continent.

The goal

As a technology company that operates internationally, the logo should reflect the company's global reach. For the logo to fit into the technology niche, it should be simple, modern, and memorable.

My role and Responsibilities

Examining the design brief, conducting research, and assembling a mood-board. idea sketches, logo design, stationary design, and brand identity development.

Logo construction

The logo is designed to be abstract and simple in appearance. The logo concept is inspired by the typical African pattern shown on the mood board, and it is a combination of the company name initial letters "r," "c," and "t," as well as an icon that depicts a desktop computer to represent technology.
