The Work Flow

  • 1

We will talk

A face-to-face or virtual meeting will be scheduled to discuss your business and expected outcomes. As a result, we will be able to decide which plan to choose, identify any unique situations that may arise, and determine final deliverables.

    •

  • 2

Research

As soon as the plan and direction have been agreed upon, I will conduct extensive research to determine the appropriate style, design, color, and overall feeling of your new design and development directions.

    •

  • 3

Direction and Excution

Following the completion of the research, I will create a mood board that illustrates the design direction, and we will discuss it together. Once you're satisfied with the design's style, look, and feel. It is time for me to begin executing.

  • 4

Revision

When the design is complete, it's time to evaluate the results. By doing this, we can identify any final touch-ups that need to be made. The final design will be presented to you for your feedback, and I will make any necessary changes.

  • 5

Delivery

As soon as I implement the feedback discussed and make sure we meet the goals set at the beginning of the project, I will deliver all the final deliverables.
