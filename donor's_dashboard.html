Love in Action Donor's Dashboard

  • Dashboard Design
  • UI/UX Design
  • Web Development

Love in Action is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of children across the globe. This project involves designing a comprehensive dashboard for donors to track their contributions and the impact they make.

The Goal

To create an intuitive and user-friendly dashboard that allows donors to track their donations, view detailed information about sponsored children, and access overall statistics about the organizationâ€™s activities and impact.

My Role and Responsibilities

Examining the design brief, conducting research, creating wireframes, designing the UI, developing the front-end, and user testing.

Project Overview

The dashboard is structured to provide a seamless experience for donors, with sections for overall statistics, detailed profiles of sponsored children, donation history, and account management.

Wireframe Sketch

Initial wireframes were created to outline the structure and layout of the dashboard, ensuring a user-centric design approach.

Site Map

A comprehensive site map was developed to illustrate the hierarchy and navigation flow of the dashboard, providing a clear guide for the user experience.
